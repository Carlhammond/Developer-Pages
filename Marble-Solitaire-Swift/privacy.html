Privacy Policy

Marble Solitaire · Effective 16 June 2026 · Last updated 16 June 2026

In short: Marble Solitaire does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party services. The game works entirely offline, and everything it saves stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Marble Solitaire ("the app," "we," "our," or "us"), developed by Carl Hammond, handles your information. By using Marble Solitaire, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require or offer an account, sign-in, or registration.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party software development kits (SDKs).
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites, and the app does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA).
  • The app makes no network connections to us or anyone else for the purpose of collecting data.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

To make the game work, Marble Solitaire saves a small amount of information locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This includes:

  • Your in-progress game, so it survives closing and reopening the app.
  • Game settings and preferences.
  • Gameplay statistics such as best results, recent game history, and active play time.

This information never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party. Deleting the app removes this data.

3. How Information Is Used

The locally stored information described above is used solely to provide the game's features — saving your progress, remembering your preferences, and showing your statistics. It is not used for any other purpose.

4. Sharing

Because we do not collect any data, we have nothing to sell, rent, trade, or share with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Marble Solitaire is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 because we do not collect data from any user.

6. Your Rights

Privacy regulations such as the GDPR (European Union) and the CCPA (California) grant you rights over your personal data. Because Marble Solitaire collects and stores no personal data on our systems, there is no personal information for us to access, correct, export, or delete. You remain in full control of the on-device game data, which you can clear at any time by resetting your statistics within the app or by deleting the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices ever change, we will update this Privacy Policy and revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
